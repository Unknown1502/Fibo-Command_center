# Apple Brand Guidelines - Product Photography

## Brand Standards
**Version:** 3.1  
**Department:** Product Marketing  
**Classification:** Confidential

## Core Photography Principles

### Simplicity First
Apple's visual language is defined by simplicity, clarity, and elegance.

### Camera Angles
- **REQUIRED:** Eye level (primary standard)
- **APPROVED:** High angle for flat lay product arrangements
- **PROHIBITED:** Dutch angles, extreme perspectives

### Lighting Standards
- **REQUIRED:** Soft, even studio lighting
- **APPROVED:** Subtle rim lighting for depth
- **PROHIBITED:** Hard shadows, dramatic lighting

### Color Palette
- **REQUIRED:** Neutral tones (white, silver, space gray)
- **APPROVED:** Subtle color accents from products
- **PROHIBITED:** Vibrant or saturated colors

### Composition Guidelines
- **PRIMARY:** Centered, symmetrical layouts
- **APPROVED:** Minimalist negative space (70%+ white space)
- **PROHIBITED:** Busy backgrounds, cluttered compositions

### Visual Style
- **REQUIRED:** Minimalist aesthetic
- **CHARACTERISTICS:** Clean, precise, premium, sophisticated
- **PROHIBITED:** Artistic interpretation, vintage effects, busy designs

## Technical Requirements

### Resolution & Quality
- Minimum: 6K resolution (6144x3456)
- Color space: Display P3 wide color gamut
- Bit depth: 16-bit per channel
- File format: TIFF or PNG (lossless)

### Background Standards
- Pure white (#FFFFFF) or subtle gradient
- No textures or patterns
- Clean, seamless integration

### Product Representation
- Products must be perfectly aligned
- No fingerprints, dust, or imperfections
- Accurate color reproduction
- Sharp focus across entire product

## Brand Values in Imagery

Every image should communicate:
- Innovation through simplicity
- Premium quality and craftsmanship
- User-focused design
- Environmental consciousness

## Prohibited Elements
- Clutter or visual noise
- Dramatic shadows
- Over-processed effects
- Competitive products in frame
- Dated or trendy aesthetics

**Approval Required:** All marketing imagery  
**Contact:** visualstandards@apple.com
