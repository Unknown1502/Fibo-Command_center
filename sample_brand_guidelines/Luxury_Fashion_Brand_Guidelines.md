# Luxury Fashion Brand Guidelines - Editorial Photography

## Brand Identity
**Brand:** Prestige Couture  
**Version:** 1.5  
**Classification:** Internal Use Only

## Editorial Photography Standards

### Visual Philosophy
Our imagery embodies timeless elegance, sophistication, and aspirational lifestyle.

### Camera Angles
- **PREFERRED:** Eye level and high angle for editorial elegance
- **APPROVED:** Low angle for architectural and venue shots
- **AVOID:** Extreme or unconventional angles

### Lighting Requirements
- **PRIMARY:** Golden hour natural lighting
- **SECONDARY:** Soft backlit for ethereal quality
- **APPROVED:** Controlled studio lighting for products
- **PROHIBITED:** Hard, harsh lighting

### Color Standards
- **SIGNATURE PALETTE:** Muted and pastel tones
- **BRAND COLORS:** Champagne, Blush Pink, Pearl White, Charcoal
- **APPROVED:** Monochrome for timeless appeal
- **PROHIBITED:** Vibrant, saturated colors

### Composition
- **REQUIRED:** Rule of thirds
- **APPROVED:** Symmetrical for product details
- **STYLE:** Balanced, elegant, spacious
- **PROHIBITED:** Chaotic or overly dynamic layouts

### Visual Style
- **PRIMARY:** Cinematic quality
- **CHARACTERISTICS:** Vintage-inspired, artistic, sophisticated
- **MOOD:** Dreamy, elegant, refined, exclusive
- **PROHIBITED:** Realistic/casual, overly modern, minimalist

## Technical Specifications

### Image Quality
- Resolution: Minimum 8K for print campaigns
- Color space: Adobe RGB (wide gamut)
- Bit depth: 16-bit required
- Format: RAW or TIFF

### Post-Processing
- Subtle film grain texture approved
- Gentle vignetting for focus
- Soft focus effects for dreamlike quality
- Color grading: Warm, muted tones

## Subject Guidelines

### Model Standards
- Elegant poses and gestures
- Natural, refined expressions
- Diverse representation
- Age range: 25-45 for primary campaigns

### Environment
- Luxury venues (boutiques, hotels, estates)
- Natural outdoor settings (gardens, coastlines)
- Architectural elements
- Minimal urban environments

## Brand Values

Visual content must communicate:
- Timeless elegance
- Exceptional craftsmanship
- Exclusive lifestyle
- Sophisticated taste
- Heritage and quality

## Compliance

### Mandatory Reviews
- Creative Director approval required
- Brand consistency check
- Technical quality verification

### Prohibited Content
- Fast fashion aesthetics
- Street style photography
- Over-edited HDR effects
- Trendy filters or effects
