# Nike Brand Guidelines - Visual Content Standards

## Brand Identity
**Version:** 2.0  
**Last Updated:** December 2025  
**Department:** Global Creative Services

## Photography Standards

### Camera Angles
- **REQUIRED:** Low angle shots to convey power and athleticism
- **APPROVED:** Eye level for product detail shots
- **PROHIBITED:** High angle shots (diminishes subject power)

### Lighting Requirements
- **PRIMARY:** Hard lighting with strong shadows for athletic drama
- **SECONDARY:** Golden hour for outdoor lifestyle shots
- **PROHIBITED:** Soft studio lighting (too gentle for brand identity)

### Color Palette
- **APPROVED:** Vibrant, saturated colors
- **BRAND COLORS:** Black (#000000), White (#FFFFFF), Orange (#FF6B35)
- **PROHIBITED:** Muted or pastel tones

### Composition Rules
- **REQUIRED:** Dynamic diagonal compositions
- **APPROVED:** Rule of thirds for product shots
- **PROHIBITED:** Static centered compositions

### Style Guidelines
- **REQUIRED:** Realistic with high energy
- **APPROVED:** Athletic, action-oriented, powerful
- **PROHIBITED:** Vintage, minimalist, soft aesthetics

## Technical Specifications

### Image Quality
- Minimum resolution: 4K (3840x2160)
- Color space: sRGB or DCI-P3
- Bit depth: 16-bit minimum for archival

### Subject Matter
- Athletes in motion
- Close-ups of product technology features
- Urban sports environments
- Diverse representation required

## Compliance Requirements

All visual content must:
1. Feature Nike products prominently
2. Convey athletic excellence and determination
3. Use approved color palette
4. Follow composition guidelines
5. Meet technical specifications

**Contact:** brandcompliance@nike.com  
**Approval Required:** All campaign imagery
